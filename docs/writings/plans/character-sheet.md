# character sheet
name
gender
birthdate
height
weight

one page backstory/data sheet (for the actual char sheet)

how they portray themselves:
- style 
    - what do they look like day to day
    - superhero outfit and name
- demeanour/personality
    - how do they come off to a stranger on the street/close friend in private time?

ancestry
profession
interests/likes/hobbies
pet peeves/dislikes/weaknesses/flaws
motivations/goals -> What and who do you wanna be?
bonds/relationships -> friends, family, enemies, lovers, rivals, acquaintances, networks

special abilities -> innate/gifted abilities that set them apart
domains of expertise -> trained areas, can and should be related to special abilties

every single leaf point above should have an explanation for why or why not.
for example, it is interesting to have a rival, and it is interesting to explain why you dont have a rival, but it is not interesting to say you dont have a rival without elaboration.
