structure of session 0:
- each player (including me) takes the hot seat for one hour.
    - the player in the hot seat must roleplay as their character as they are directly before the campaign starts.
        - they must reply to all questions in character, as part of one hour-long ongoing conversation.
        - they can only break character to respond to suggestions from other players and must return to character before the next question
        - should reply to anything else in the conversation (small talk etc) in character
    - the other players (including me) will challenge the player in the hot seat with either questions or suggestions.
         - what questions? try to ask for interesting things like:
            - what is your name?
            - where are you from?
            - which three of your friends would you bring to a desert island?
            - why are you a superhero?
            - try to ask for an answer to the ultimate question: why should i be interested in this character?
        - the suggestions 
            - should be about how they could change one of their previous answers or new assumptions about the world slightly
        - by the end of the hour, all players at the table must be:
            - interested in each individual character beyond just a side character to their own
    - one player will be the bookkeeper and document all the information being verbally communicated
        - i will be the bookkeeper unless i am in the hotseat
    - when I am in the hotseat, the other players should question me about the world or suggest things to add, remove, or modify
        - can be anything relevant, like related to your planned character progression or totally random
        - aim to dig deeper and force us to come up with interesting and relevant things about the world
