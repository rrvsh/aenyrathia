structure of session 0:
- each player (including me) takes the hot seat for one hour.
    - the player in the seat will speak about their character and field questions about the character or the world.
    - the other players (including me) will challenge the player in the hot seat with either questions or suggestions.
        - the suggestions should try to build on the intended player fantasy and to make the narrative more interesting
        - the questions should aim to punish and eradicate any vagueness in the hot seat's player story.
        - by the end of the hour, all players at the table must be in complete alignment on that character.
    - one player will be the bookkeeper and document all the information being verbally communicated
        - i will be the bookkeeper unless i am in the hotseat
    - when I am in the hotseat, the other players should question me about the world or suggest things to add, remove, or modify
        - can be anything relevant, like related to your planned character progression or totally random
        - aim to dig deeper and force us to come up with interesting and relevant things about the world
