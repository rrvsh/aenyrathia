Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition (from 2014) was what [we](The Group.md),
started out playing, purely because [I](The DM.md) suggested it. I preferred it for a campaign. It was the 
easiest thing to breakdown into playable smaller chunks of the rules, so it was 
the easiest thing I could teach with my preferred iterative teaching method of 
giving a student a small thing and slowly adding more to it.

My players will protest as me saying that does imply that I had to teach them
the in and outs of DnD, but two of my three core players (at the time of 
writing), Haikal and Toph, were indeed not new players but instead had extensive 
experience with DnD. I will counter this first by saying that there are a few
reasons why this is not the real reason why and it was because I suggested it.
