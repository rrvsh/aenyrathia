# Ampere

Nicholas Zivmen is a 28 year old electrical engineer who was working at a Ilrinian power plant when an incursion occured, causing him to fall into a ruptured generator, mutating him into a monstrous creature with metal skin and two protrusions from his back that let him conduct and discharge electricity. His mutation causes significant nerve pain when he uses his abilities, but gives him a sense of euphoria that makes him addicted to using them. He serves as the top bruiser for EchoTech's Division IX, supporting Arma and Artillery on their R&D into the incursions.
