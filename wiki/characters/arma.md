# Arma

Max Levin is a human in his 30s that has just joined EchoTech's Divison IX after being mutated after an incursion at the Ilrinian Central Zoo.
He has developed a hard keratin shell over his body that he can manipulate and project.
He's a shy and reclusive person, especially after his mutation. He prefers to keep to himself and his work.
He wants to find a reversal to his condition, which is why he is readily dove into the work of R&D into the incursions.
He's the lead researcher into the Vasir at D:IX.
