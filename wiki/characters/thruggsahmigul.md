# Thruggsahmigul

Thruggsahmigul, or Sahm for short, is a young orc man living in Ilrinia. He grew up in an EchoTech mining colony in the Pig Iron Sea, as part of a tribe of storytellers. After an early incursion wiped out his tribe, he developed the superpower to walk leylines. He has been working with Xen Myers and Ascanius Morzansson at Xepyhr.net
