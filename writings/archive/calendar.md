# Calendar

Aenyrathia used to follow the Aenyrathian Luminary Calendar, but has switched to using the New Aenyrathian Calendar ever since the Awakened introduced it after the Incident.

## New Aenyrathian Calendar

Dates are written as [EPOCH] <YEAR>.<MONTH>.<DAY>.

Epochs are two letter codes representing the era the year is in. For now, there are only two: BA (Before Awakening) and AA (After Awakening).

Years are zero-based after AA, while months and days are 1 based. There are twelve months in the calender, each with 30 days.

AA 0.1.1 maps to the 10th of Verdantia 1892, or the 10th day of the 11th month of the 1892nd year of the Luminary Calendar.
Before AA 0.1.1, incrementing the year counts backwards. For example, BI 10.10.4 is the fourth day of the tenth month ten years before the incident.
