# Artillery

Arthur O'Leary is a 24 year old human who was horribly mutated after an explosion in an orichalcum mine, causing his lungs to grow tenfold, absorbing the ambient mana and causing his bodily fluids to become acidic and volatile. The mutation causes extreme pain to him, and he is only able to function due to the powerful painkillers EchoTech developed just for his condition. This has led him to join Divison IX to work with Arma on a cure, lending his expertise in geochemistry.

## Appearance

Artillery wears a suit with tubes that constantly inject painkillers into him, that he has recently modified to allow injecting different chemicals into his acid stream.
