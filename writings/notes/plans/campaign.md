echo tech takes over in a big way

### breaking the barrier

in the wake of the seraphs breaking the extraplanar barrier, few things happen:
- arcane resonance i.e. latent magic power in the weave goes wayyy up
- extraplanar incursions begin appearing EXTREMELY frequently and all over the
globe, everywhere. they vary in size, with only the big ones needing military or
police defense. however, the smaller ones still hurt.

#### orichalcum nanoparticles

as a result of the incursions, orichalcum nanoparticles that the portals emit
begin being detected in aenyrathian autopsies around AA 0.1, after a mining
incident in Ibuth.

the combination of the orichalcum nanoparticles being in people now + the arcane
resonance rising leads to some rare people, who already had a strong response to
arcane resonance already (modern magicians), developing rare and unique powers.
research is still going on about this phenomenon and no one truly knows anything
about these beings, though governments are scrambling.

the party are not the first people who developed powers, but they are the first
people in their city and the news papers in the city are on edge to find out who
it could be.

mosskach already found theirs and mobilised them against the revolution of
sector 7. most of the world's other cities that have had people with powers have
either had them go missing or join the government to help fight bigger
incursions.

the party will be the FIRST PEOPLE WITH POWERS TO HAVE SECRET IDENTITIES! they
will also be the origin of the trend, which will slowly spread throughout the
world.

therefore, the party characters must:
1. have a well-defined power set at the beginning of the campaign. this should
be simple, and limited to one to three "types" of powers. examples would be year
one spiderman or batman, or even a bit more abstract, like a minor gang leader
who has rumours about his abilities.
2. have a reason they want to keep their identities secret. you must have
something you care about or want to protect, other than just saying you want to
have a secret identity so it doesnt affect your daily life (aka like a
celebrity). if your secret identity is revealed, it could potentially be the
equivalent of LIFE-THREATENING!

#### gods

gods in this campaign will be treated not as religions, but as manifestations of
the concepts of civilisation and of nature. we will likely focus on new gods,
assuming that arche is continuing investigation into old gods with one of the
archmages.

new gods are going to all be essentially the concepts that form as a result of
civilisation i.e. what happens when humans live very densely.

for e.g. digital surveillance was at an all time high because echotech had their
systems in every city in the entire world, and was monitoring them at some
level. this caused aleph-null, the new god of digital surveillance to form, just
like many new gods have been forming, but the only reason he was able to
manifest was because saziver was 1. a powerful enough psion to break the
extraplanar barrier (spoilers!) and 2. a HUGE believer in digital surveillance.

all this to say, we will be having a lot more new gods, either borne out of the
stronger cconcepts that emerge in this time period, or already having existed
but now able to manifest, and a lot more worship of those gods.

the worship will again not really be the same as the typical organised religion
in dnd, and will not be focused on organisation or hierarchy (which is what
arche is doing), but rather on these concepts in humanity and exploring the
drives and effects of them.

how they will develop (and you guys can do this too in character) is when modern
people not only start noticing these minor occurrences, except they feel like
their hand was divinely guided.

this will most likely be the b plot of each session!
