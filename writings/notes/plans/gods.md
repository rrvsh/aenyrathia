### god of investigation

#### an excerpt from the notes of a harried biographermantist in AA 91 writing
about the bard class

a lot more occurrence of newsworthy events -> newspapers cant keep up. demand
goes up sharply, so supply does too. the number of online blogs, ham radio
stations, scrolls delivered through messenger pigeons, and a rise in so-called
"bards" - these storytellers began to crop up around AA 0.12 to AA 1.1 and would
perform anywhere, any time, seeking out stories when they werent in hunt for the
best to perform - all rose in the wake of the Incident Involving the Extraplanar
Resonator of AA -1.

#### first emergence

as more interesting stories began happening across aenyrathia in the wake of the
extraplanar barrier breaking in AA 0, so did the demand for more coverage of
these stories, with even local news stores being overwhelmed.

with this demand, "guerilla" ways of spreading news started becoming more
common. some turned to analog methods, distributing their stories in print and
via ham radio, or for the more arcane, through [ghostly messenger pigeons
deposting scrolls direct from author to reader](superheroes/ghost-bird-bard.md)
others leaned into the technology of their generation, spreading their writings
on online blogs and web serials.

what every single one of the people involved in this phenomena had, though, was
a keen passion, interest, and active participation in **investigation** - their
thirst and ability to firstly seek out questions, and the answers to them.

the rise of this [[Micro-Media Renaissance]] led to a new force awakening in the
world, one borne out of that thirst for journalism.
